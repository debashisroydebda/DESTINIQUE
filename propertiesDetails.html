Designed for the modern thinker who appreciates comfort in style, the 2-bedroom condominium at The Pointe provides 1200+ square feet of space to relax in true luxury. Greeted with hardwood floors, chic yet beachy art, and sleek design, the 2-bedroom condominium leads you through an open floor plan where you and your guests can enjoy your company in the living room, while preparing delicious food in the high-end stainless steel kitchen.

Both the living room and kitchen provide the Emerald Coast sunshine with doors leading to the balcony or patio. The master bedroom comes with an office nook if you are inclined to do some work while visiting The Pointe, and if you’d rather relax, this bedroom is equipped with a flat screen television, bathroom with bathtub and separate shower, and private entrance onto the balcony or patio.

The Pointe is a non-smoking resort - no smoking on property. This includes no smoking in units, on balconies, or in any common area. Smoking permitted off the property or in the parking lot as long as the guest is at least 50 feet away from the building.
